Detroit Metropolitan Airport Wifi Landing Page

Description

The client presented me with the old, outdated model of the splash page, to wchich they wanted me to come up with a sleeker, more modern version. I tackled this by integrating modern design principles including rounded corners, buttons replacing links, and dark mode features.
