"Read-A-Rhyme" Application

This was a two-semester long team capstone project where my team worked with a real client to create a functional product that met the company's requirements. The purpose behind this project was to produce software that solves a complex problem through the analysis of design tradeoffs, non-functional requirements, and real-world customer needs.


We incorporated iterative development, design prototyping (via Figma), along with user research. Regarding the software development, I was tasked with the design and implementation of the Front End components. I used interaction principles that best suited our target audience (children ages 4 - 6). Check out the design prototypes for the different interaction modes below!
