Computational Media

Often times, there is a little bit of ambiguity and confusion from employers/clients about what exactly "Computational Media" is, so I would like to elaborate on my major a little bit more. Computational media (CM) is a branch of computer science (CS); it is the "sister" major to CS here at Georgia Tech. CM is a combination of computer science and design, with creative applications such as front end development, user experience/user interaction, and game design. Essentially, computer science focuses on backend whereas computational media is half backend, half front end with the incorporation of design principles. I personally love it because it gives a creative outlet while I can still solve the puzzle-like challenges that coding presents.
